Statistical Infographics

This project represents a highly advanced synthesis of thematic cartography, quantitative data visualization, and commercial graphic design. The core challenge involved communicating dense, complex datasets through a singular, cohesive, multi-element poster.


This infographic tells the story of Canada's place in the clean energy era through a combination of maps and statistical visualizations. The layout was rigorously executed in Adobe Illustrator, restricting typographic variance and engineering a press-ready CMYK document suitable for large-format commercial printing.

Cartogram Generation
Generated customized cartograms, purposefully distorting boundaries to visualize statistical variables.
Forces viewer to confront data spatially, making abstract disparities visually apparent.
Comprehensive Visualization
Designed and integrated diverse statistical graphics into a cohesive narrative structure.
Prevents cognitive overload by utilizing varied visual metaphors for lay audiences.
Typographic Hierarchy
Applied rigorous design principles, restricting the design to a maximum of three type families.
Instills strict discipline, ensuring text guides the eye without contributing to visual clutter.
Narrative Formulation
Formulated a distinct theme, blending statistical data and imagery into a unified tool.
Transforms raw data points into a compelling visual argument tailored to the current geopolitical context.
Pre-Press Formatting
Engineered final files for commercial production (A1, CMYK, bleeds, printer's marks).
Guarantees flawless reproduction of rich colors and edge-to-edge graphics in physical media.
