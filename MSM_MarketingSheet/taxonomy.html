Multi-Scale Map Taxonomy

This project involved engineering a comprehensive multi-scale taxonomy chart for a Canadian Arctic Topographic and Physical Geography Basemap. Anticipating rapid regional changes driven by climate shifts and economic development, the objective was to design a scalable, universally applicable foundation that could integrate seamlessly with future specialized overlays, such as bathymetry or mineral surveys.


The workflow required establishing strict display hierarchies for variable zoom levels, prioritizing human-recognizable scale intervals over exacting pixel densities. A specialized cool, muted color palette inspired by alpine cartography was developed to ensure immediate geographic recognition and thematic cohesion across all scales.
