Hazardous Decisions: Crisis Cartography

This project simulates a critical, time-sensitive emergency scenario involving a freight train derailment and an explosive hazard in the Halifax Regional Municipality. Acting as a GIS analyst, the objective was to immediately generate actionable spatial intelligence for first responders. Operating within ArcGIS Pro, the workflow required the rapid synthesis and projection (WKID 2961) of disparate municipal datasets.


The analysis utilized advanced proximity geoprocessing to establish 1524m evacuation buffers and network routing tools to calculate precision transit times to critical facilities. The culmination was four specialized map layouts engineered to facilitate instantaneous decision-making in high-pressure environments.
