H3 in QGIS: Spatial Trends

This project focused on advanced geovisualization techniques within an open-source environment, specifically utilizing QGIS to identify and communicate spatial distributions. The objective was to transform dense, overlapping point data into coherent visual narratives that expose underlying geographic phenomena.


Through the rigorous application of Heatmap, Hotspot, and Hexbin (H3) rendering strategies, USGS earthquake data was distilled into accessible cartographic products. The workflow required extensive exploratory data analysis, the implementation of data reduction methodologies, and the optimization of final map layouts to support data-driven storytelling.
